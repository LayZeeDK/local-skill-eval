## Decisions Made

- **qwen3.5:4b already meets target:** The 300s target is met at 235s avg. Remaining plans in 04.1 focus on further optimization and documenting what knobs are effective.
- **Per-trial execution in benchmark runner:** Changed from batch `--trials=N` to individual `--trials=1` calls to avoid a single timeout killing all trials. Each trial gets its own 600s timeout.
- **Profiler uses temp workspace:** Copies task files to `.tmp-profile-workspace/` to avoid modifying the real task directory during profiling.
